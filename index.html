解决Ubuntu中文乱码

date: 2015-04-17 15:36:25

tags: “Ubuntu疑难杂症”

Setp1:
修改/var/lib/locales/supported.d/local内容为zh_CN.UTF-8 UTF-8

Setp2:
修改/etc/default/locale内容为:
LANG=”zh_CN.UTF-8”
LANGUAGE=”zh_CN:”

Setp3:
执行sudo locale-gen
